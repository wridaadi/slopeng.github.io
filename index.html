Slopeng Beach

Slopeng Beach is one of the North Beaches in Sumenep besides Lombang Beach, this beach is located in Dasuk District, 21 km from Sumenep City Center. This beach has its own characteristic, namely the stretch of sand which is quite mountainous decorated with siwalan trees, coconut trees and shrimp pine trees, so that tourists can enjoy the beauty of the northern coast of Madura from the gentle hills.

Location

Slopeng Beach, Sumenep Regency is located in East Java province on Ambunten No.34, Ambunten, Trebung Ghetteng, Sema"an, Dasuk, Sumenep Regency, East Java, Indonesia

Traveling Time

Travel time from Suramadu to Slopeng Beach is around 5-6 hours. If departing from the west direction of Madura, in addition to the southern route, you can also pass the north coast side path

Opened

Visitors can enjoy the beauty of Slopeng Beach from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. every day. On certain days there will be traditional art performances on the Slopeng Beach pavilion.

Entrance Ticket

You are only charged an entrance ticket of IDR 5.000 each person. If you bring a vehicle, you will be charged a parking fee of IDR 5.000 for a motorcycle and IDR 10.000 for a car.

Tourist Attraction

Some recommendations at Slopeng Beach such as swimming, horse riding on the beach, taking a photo in sand hill, culinary tourism along the beach, and hunting for sunset.

Nearest Hotel

Popular hotels near Slopeng Beach such as Wijaya I&II Hotel, Family Nur Hotel, C1 Hotel, Adhirasa House Sumenep, Dubai Sumenep Hotel, Nata Homestay, Mitraland Hotel, etc.

Facilities

Some facilities are provided at Slopeng Beach to care tourist activities.

Gazebo

Visitors can enjoy the beautiful Slopeng Beach through the provided gazebos.

Playground

Slopeng Beach is equipped with playground facilities such as swing boards and slides.

Restroom

This tourism place provides restrooms that can be used to clean body and change clothes.

Parking Area

Visitors can leave their vehicles in the parking area that has been provided.

Horse Rental

By riding a horse, visitors can get around the beach without feeling tired.

Art Stage

Visitors can see traditional Madurese art performances that be held at certain times.

Food Court & Souvenir Shop

There are some handcraft souvenir shop and food courts in the coast that sells Madurese foods such as rujak, sate, and soto.

Rest Area

visitors can take a rest and having lunch on the provided rest area.

Shrimp Pine Tree

The green shrimp pine trees that grow on the beach keep history for local people.
